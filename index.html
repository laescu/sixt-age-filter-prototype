Sub-row strip
Age + corporate rate in a muted strip below fields

Both age filter and corporate rate sit together in a compact grey strip spanning the full IBE width — directly below the main fields row. Inspired by the Avis IBE ("Driver's Age | Residency | Apply Wizard Number"). Minimal visual weight but logically grouped and easy to discover.

Low friction Moderate compliance Homepage

Both secondary actions share one consistent location. Users who need to adjust age or enter a corporate code find them in the same strip without hunting through the form or a hidden footer link.
